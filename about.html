About UBCICT

University of Botswana Computer science society for Innovative and Communications Technology

University of Botswana Computer science society for Innovative and Communications Technology is a club established in 2010.The main focus of the club is to obtain,apply and distribute computing knowledge and skills within the University of Botswna student community.

Our Goals

Our aim is to
  • Register many new members into the club
  • Inform members and the public about tech events happening the country or globaly
  • Promote research skills among students

Mission

To promote the awareness of IT through outreach programmes and provide accademic assistance and networking opportunity for students

Values

  • Honesty
  • Hardworking
  • Responsible
  • Determination
  • Creativity
  • Energetic
